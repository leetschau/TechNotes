See usage mannul at [rebar wiki](https://github.com/rebar/rebar/wiki);

Or you can Download rebar manually at [rebar homepage](https://github.com/basho/rebar);

Basic usage:

1. Download rebar to project root directory;

1. `./rebar create-app`

1. edit source files;

1. `./rebar compile`
